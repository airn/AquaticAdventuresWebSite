Aquatic Adventures

Serving The Worlds Most Beautiful Beaches

BANANA BOAT

Takes riding a banana to a whole new level! You and 5 of your friends can enjoy a 2 mile ride around the beautiful Gulf of Mexico. Better hold on tight!

DOLPHIN TOURS

Follow our guides around the beautiful shell island and swim with dolphins on one of guided shell island tours. You can choose to explore aboard a boat driven by one of our certified captains or drive your own jet-ski.

WAVERUNNERS

Rip up the Gulf of Mexico on our Jet-Skis from 30 minutes and up. Adrenaline junkies get your fix in while you are on vacation.

PONTOON BOATS

Enjoy Shell Island with up to 11 of your friends and family with a Pontoon Boat. Pack a lunch and a cooler, leave from our marina location, and captain your own boat to enjoy a half or full day out on the water.
