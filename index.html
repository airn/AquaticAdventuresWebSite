Aquatic Adventures

Serving The Worlds Most Beautiful Beaches

BANANA BOAT

Banana boat rides are perfect for cooling off after a hot morning on the beach. This is a fun and easy ride for the whole family.

DOLPHIN TOURS

Follow our guides around the beautiful shell island and swim with dolphins on one of guided shell island tours. You can choose to explore aboard a boat driven by one of our certified captains or drive your own jet-ski.

WAVE RUNNERS

Rip up the Gulf of Mexico on our Jet-Skis from 30 minutes and up. Adrenaline junkies get your fix in while you are on vacation.

PONTOON BOATS

Pontoon boat rentals are a main attraction. Boat rentals at Aquatic Adventures of Panama City Beach are a great way to experience all sorts of things.. A few quick pointers and your your venture begins.

PARASAILING

Aquatic Adventures will now be booking Parasail for Paradise Watersports.

Sail Boats

Rent a Sailboat and cruise the Beach from the water. At selected sites only.

Dolphin Express

Come Aboard our 60ft EXPRESS sightseeing Dolphin Tour Boat that seats 90 passengers. Our friendly staff will provide you with an informative tour that is safe and fun for all ages.

Paddleboards

On the nice, calm days there is nothing better to do than paddle around the Gulf on one of our GUSU Paddleboards. If you're lucky the native sea life will join you.

Fish Now!

Panama City Beach deep sea fishing at its best. Come fish on your own private trip just for you and your group. Great for families and kids or a small corporate gataway. Come aboard a charter that offers years of experience fishing off the Florida Coast.
